About Vichy Catalan

Since 1881, Vichy Catalan has been a symbol of natural health and refreshment. Originating from a privileged spring in Caldes de Malavella, Girona, its waters are meticulously guarded and bottled directly from the source, ensuring every drop retains its pristine qualities.

Our commitment extends beyond providing exceptional mineral water. We are dedicated to educating our consumers about the unique properties and benefits of natural mineral water, fostering a deeper understanding of hydration and wellness.

From its distinct flavor to its therapeutic properties, Vichy Catalan is more than just water; it's a legacy of health, taste, and natural purity passed down through generations.
