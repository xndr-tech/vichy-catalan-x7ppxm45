Why Choose Vichy Catalan?

Natural Mineral Composition

Sourced from the thermal spring of Caldes de Malavella in Girona, Spain, Vichy Catalan is naturally rich in essential minerals like bicarbonate, sodium, potassium, and magnesium, contributing to its distinctive taste and potential health benefits.

Distinctive Taste & Fine Bubbles

Experience a refreshing and unique flavor profile, characterized by its naturally integrated fine bubbles and a slightly salty, complex taste that sets it apart from ordinary sparkling waters. It's an ideal companion for gourmet meals.

The Lithium Question (Busted!)

Concerned about lithium? While Vichy Catalan does contain trace amounts of natural lithium (approx. 1.2 mg per liter), you'd need to drink roughly 4,000 liters (or 8,000 half-liter bottles) *daily* for an extended period to even *begin* to approach therapeutic levels, let alone toxic ones. So, enjoy your Vichy Catalan without a worry!

Our Heritage, Your Hydration

Since 1881, Vichy Catalan has been a symbol of quality and natural wellness. Discovered by Dr. Modest Furest i Roca, the thermal waters of Caldes de Malavella have been revered for centuries. Today, Vichy Catalan continues to be bottled directly from its protected source, maintaining its original purity and unique mineral balance.
